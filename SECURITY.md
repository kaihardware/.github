# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Do not open a public issue. Use GitHub Private Vulnerability Reporting when enabled, or email **security@kaihardware.com** with the affected product/version, impact, reproduction steps, and safe contact details. Replace this address with a monitored mailbox before publication.

We aim to acknowledge reports within 3 business days and provide an initial assessment within 10 business days. Timelines are targets, not a warranty. We will coordinate remediation and disclosure in good faith.

## Scope

Security includes software, firmware, connected services, update mechanisms, hardware interfaces, debug ports, supply-chain artifacts, and privacy-impacting behavior. Safety-critical hazards should also be escalated to the product owner immediately.

## Researcher expectations

Avoid privacy violations, data destruction, service disruption, physical harm, and access beyond what demonstrates the issue. Give us reasonable time to fix before disclosure.
