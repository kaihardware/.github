# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We commit to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community regardless of background, identity, experience, or ability.

## Expected behavior

Be constructive and specific; critique ideas and artifacts, not people; distinguish fact, assumption, and opinion; respect privacy and confidential work; disclose conflicts; and accept responsibility for mistakes.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, doxxing, sustained disruption, retaliation, or publishing private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to **conduct@kaihardware.com**. Organization owners will acknowledge reports, limit disclosure, investigate proportionately, avoid conflicts of interest, and document outcomes. Maintainers may edit or remove content, warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently ban participants. Replace this address with a monitored mailbox before publication.

This policy applies in repositories, discussions, events, and private interactions representing KAI.
